Bo Peng

I am a Ph.D. student and research assistant in Geographic Information Science (GIScience) at University of Wisconsin - Madison, working with Prof. Qunying Huang.

I've been thinking about leveraging machine learning and computer vision to advance geospatial data science.

Previously, I was a research assistant at National Engineering Laboratory for Satellite Remote Sensing Applications (NELRS), Institute of Remote Sensing and Digital Earth (now as Aerospace Information Research Institute), Chinese Academy of Sciences. I received the second M.Sc. in ECE/Machine Learning from UW-Madison (2019), the first M.Sc. in GIScience/Remote Sensing from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (2017) (advisor: Prof. Qingxi Tong and Prof. Lifu Zhang), and the B.Eng. in Remote Sensing from Wuhan University (2014).
