About

Dr. Bo Peng is a Senior Research Scientist with PAII, the U.S. research lab of Ping An Technology. He worked as an AI resident with X, the Moonshot Factory (formerly Google[x]) in Fall 2021, a Research Scientist Intern with PAII in Summer 2021, and a Research Intern with Los Alamos National Laboratory (LANL) in Summer 2020.

He received his Ph.D., majoring in Geography (Remote Sensing) and minoring in Electrical Engineering (Machine Learning) at Spatial Computing and Data Mining Lab, University of Wisconsin - Madison (UW-Madison), working with Dr. Qunying Huang, the M.Sc. from Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering at UW-Madison, the M.Sc. from Institute of Remote Sensing and Digital Earth, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the B.E. from School of Remote Sensing and Information Engineering, Wuhan University.

His research interest lies at the intersection of remote sensing, computer vision, and natural hazards.
