I am a Ph.D. student in Geographic Information Science (GIScience) at University of Wisconsin - Madison, advised by Prof. Qunying Huang.

I've been thinking about leveraging machine learning and computer vision to advance remote sensing and GIScience for disaster resilience.

Previously, I received the M.Sc. from Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (Machine Leanring track) at UW-Madison (2019), the M.Sc. from State Key Laboratory of Remote Sensing Science, Institute of Remote Sensing and Digital Earth (now as Aerospace Information Research Institute), Chinese Academy of Sciences (2017) (advisor: Prof. Qingxi Tong and Prof. Lifu Zhang), and the B.Eng. from School of Remote Sensing and Information Enginnering, Wuhan University (2014).
