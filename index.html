About

Bo is a Ph.D. student in Geographic Information Science with doctoral minor in Electrical Engineering (Machine Learning) at Department of Geography, University of Wisconsin - Madison, working with Prof. Qunying Huang.

He, as the principal investigator (PI), is awarded the Microsoft AI for Earth Grant in 2020. He is working as a research intern at Los Alamos National Laboratory supported by the Applied Machine Learning Summer Research Fellowship for summer 2020.

Previously, he was a research assistant at National Engineering Laboratory for Satellite Remote Sensing Applications, Institute of Remote Sensing and Digital Earth, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He received the M.Sc. in ECE (Machine Learning track) from UW-Madison (2019), the M.Sc. in Geographic Information System from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (2017), and the B.Eng. in Remote Sensing from Wuhan University (2014).
